Interesting facts about camel

  • Camels are called as SHIP OF DESERT
  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months! He can drink up to 40 gallons in one go.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.
  • Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day.
  • Camels can travel at up to 40 miles per hour – the same as a racehorse!

Interesting facts about teddy

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
